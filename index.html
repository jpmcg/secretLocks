Secret Locks Salon Belfast

About

Secret Locks Hairdressers and Extension Specialists is one of Belfast's newest salons. Located in the busy Queens Quarter area offering a wide range of hair treatments, services and hair extensions.

Facilities

Davines Product Range

Davines appealed to us not only because of the beautiful range of high shine colours and effective styling products, but also because of the company's sustainability ethos. Using Renewable energy only - Electrical energy coming from renewable sources in their plants. Eco-Packaging - They do their utmost to use recyclable and reusable packaging.

Colour Range

Mask with Vibrachrome - An innovative colouring system using technology that is unprecedented in the cosmetics world giving hair intense conditioning power and extraordinarily shiny, long lasting colour

Styling Range

More inside - A range of specific yet versatile products that can be used on their own or mixed and layered together. They are formulated to enhance styling and finishing performance.
Essential Haircare - All products are also free from sulphates and parabens making them kinder to the hair and scalp and safe for use on hair extensions.

Secret Locks Extension Range

Sam has over 6 years experience in the hair extension industry. With her expertise you can add length, volume and colour to hair without chemicals, damage or the excessive price tag.
Providing a range of methods and extensions to suit every client's budget, the hair of your dreams is possible.
Only the finest human hair is used, with each client receiving a custom tailored fitting to suit their needs.
